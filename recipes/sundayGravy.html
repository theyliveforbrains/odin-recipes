Sunday Gravy Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 large garlic cloves, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 8 ounces sweet Italian-style sausage, casings removed
  • 1 pound ground beef sirloin
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper
  • 1 35-ounce can Italian peeled tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 28-ounce can tomato puree
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, torn into bits

Cooking Instructions

  1. In large saucepan, cook the onion and garlic in the oil over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 7 minutes.
  2. Stir in sausage meat and break up.
  3. Add beef and salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook, stirring often, until meat is nicely browned and crumbly.
  5. Tip pan and discard fat.
  6. Add tomatoes and tomato puree, then add about 1/2 cup water to each can and swirl it around to rinse out the can.
  7. Add the liquid to pan, with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Partly cover pan; bring sauce to a simmer.
  9. Reduce heat to low and cook, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thickened and oil has separated from the tomatoes, about 1 1/2 hours.
  10. Stir in the basil and cook 5 minutes more. Serve immediately or use in making lasagna.

Serving Size

Makes 8-10 servings
